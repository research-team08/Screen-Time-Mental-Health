Digital Exposure and Its Effects on Adolescent’s Mental and Physical Well-Being in Bangladesh

This research aims to explore how increasing digital exposure affects the mental and physical well-being of adolescents in Bangladesh. It focuses on understanding patterns of screen use, sleep quality, learning behavior, and emotional stability among young individuals. The study seeks to identify both the positive and negative outcomes of technology use in daily life and how parental involvement influences these effects. The findings will help raise awareness and promote healthier digital habits among adolescents.
